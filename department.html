Departments & Services

Cardiology

The Cardiology Department provides comprehensive care for all types of heart-related conditions, using advanced diagnostic tools and evidence-based treatments. Our team of experienced cardiologists, cardiac technicians, and nursing staff is dedicated to delivering accurate diagnosis, effective management, and compassionate care. We offer a full range of services including heart check-ups, ECG, echocardiography, stress tests, cardiac imaging, and emergency cardiac care.

Our goal is to ensure timely intervention, personalized treatment plans, and long-term support for patients with heart diseases, helping them maintain a healthier and more active life.

Oncology

The Oncology Department is dedicated to the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of cancer. It provides comprehensive care through medical oncology, surgical oncology, and radiation therapy services. The department offers advanced diagnostic evaluations, personalized treatment plans, chemotherapy administration, and supportive care. A multidisciplinary team of oncologists, nurses, and specialists works collaboratively to ensure precise, compassionate, and evidence-based cancer management.

Orthopedics

The Orthopedics Department offers specialized medical and surgical care for conditions involving the bones, joints, muscles, and spine. The department delivers services such as fracture management, joint replacement procedures, musculoskeletal assessments, and treatment of sports-related injuries.

Pediatrics

The Pediatrics Department provides specialized healthcare services for infants, children, and adolescents. It ensures comprehensive care through routine check-ups, immunizations, management of pediatric illnesses, and developmental monitoring under the supervision of experienced pediatricians.

Radiology

The Radiology Department is equipped with advanced imaging technologies to support accurate clinical diagnosis. Services include X-ray, ultrasound, CT scan, MRI, and other imaging modalities, with interpretations performed by qualified radiologists to ensure precise diagnostic outcomes.

Laboratory

The Laboratory Department delivers a wide range of diagnostic testing services, including hematology, biochemistry, microbiology, and pathology analyses. The department adheres to strict quality standards to provide reliable, timely, and clinically relevant laboratory results.
