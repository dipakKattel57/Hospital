Free Health Camp ‚Äî Cardiology Screening

A free cardiology screening camp was organized to promote heart health in the community. Dozens of participants received blood pressure checks, ECG tests, and expert consultations from cardiologists. The event aimed to raise awareness about early detection of heart disease and provide accessible care to those in need. Organizers expressed satisfaction with the strong turnout and plan to continue similar health initiatives in the future.
