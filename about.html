About CityCare Hospital

Founded in 2005, CityCare Hospital is a tertiary-care institution offering comprehensive services across medical and surgical specialties. Our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare.

Mission

Our mission is to deliver compassionate, patient-centered healthcare through advanced medical expertise, modern technology, and a commitment to safety and excellence. We strive to provide accessible, high-quality treatments that promote healing, comfort, and the well-being of every individual we serve.

Vision

Our vision is to become a leading healthcare institution recognized for outstanding clinical care, innovation, and patient satisfaction. We aim to build a healthier community by setting new standards in medical excellence, research, and continuous improvement.

Values

Compassion, Excellence, Integrity, Teamwork, Innovation, Patient-Centered Care, Accountability
